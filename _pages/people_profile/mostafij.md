I am a PhD student at <a href="https://www.yorku.ca/">York University</a>, Canada. My research interests lie at the intersection of Software Engineering, Large Language Models (LLMs), and AI-driven software systems, with a particular focus on software verification, AI for Software Engineering (AI4SE), and Software Engineering for AI (SE4AI).

Previously, I served as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at <a href="https://just.edu.bd/">Jashore University of Science and Technology (JUST)</a>, Bangladesh, where I am currently on study leave. I completed my MSc in Computer Science and Engineering at Kyung Hee University, South Korea, under the supervision of Prof. Young-Koo Lee at the Data and Knowledge Engineering (DKE) Lab. I obtained my BSc in Software Engineering from the Institute of Information Technology, University of Dhaka.

Before joining academia, I worked as a Software Engineer and Research Assistant across multiple institutions and industry organizations. My research spans community search, big data, distributed systems, data mining, machine learning, and software engineering, with recent emphasis on generative AI and formal verification.
