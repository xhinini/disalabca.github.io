I, a faculty member of the Faculty of Engineering, Comilla University, Bangladesh, is currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Science at the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Lassonde School of Engineering, York University, Toronto, Canada under the supervision of <a href='https://giasuddin.ca/'>Dr. Gias Uddin</a>, Associate Professor, York University, Lassonde School of Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. In 2016, I achieved a B.Sc. (Hons) in Computer Science and Engineering from Jahangirnagar University in Savar, Dhaka. In 2018, I acquired an M.Sc. from the same institution, placing me in the top 1%. From November 17 to March 19, I was employed by the Ministry of Finance's (MoF) as an Assistant Programmer at the National Board of Revenue (NBR) umder Internal Resources Division (IRD). 
My research interests include software testing, fuzzing, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and deep learning systems.
