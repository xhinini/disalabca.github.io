I am Haoran, a Ph.D. student at the <a href='https://www.yorku.ca/'>York University</a>, supervised by <a href='https://giasuddin.ca/'>Dr. Gias Uddin</a> and <a href='https://www.eecs.yorku.ca/~wangsong//'>Dr. Song Wang</a>. My current research centers on LLMs and AI for Software Engineering. I am passionate about developing reliable, effective AI-driven solutions to support and improve software development practices.
