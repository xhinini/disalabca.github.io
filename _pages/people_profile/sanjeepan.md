I am Sanjeepan Sivapiran, a graduate student pursuing MASc in Computer Engineering at the <a href='https://lassonde.yorku.ca/'>Lassonde School of Engineering</a>, <a href='https://www.yorku.ca/'>York University</a>. My research interests lie in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Software Engineering, with a particular focus on improving code generation through alignment techniques (RLAIF, DPO, RLHF) for Large Language Models.

Before joining York University, I completed my BSc Engineering (Hons) in Computer Science & Engineering with First Class at the <a href='https://uom.lk/'>University of Moratuwa</a>, Sri Lanka. I also have industry experience as a Machine Learning Engineer at <a href='https://h2o.ai/'>H2O.ai</a>.
